Google Developer Student Clubs

On October, 2020, Assigned me as a Operations Team Manager at Google Developer Student Club PHINMA UPang. Powered by Google Developers, it is a group of students from PHINMA University of Pangasinan whose aim is to bridge the gap between theory and practical application for student developers. Together with the 1,266+ other GDSC chapters in 106 countries around the world, it is part of a global community that is supported by Google Developers. GDSC PHINMA UPang is also the only university-wide organization in PHINMA UPang that is officially recognized by Google. This community that I started is successfully organized multiple events that help aspiring developers get to know and learn the different Google developer technologies available to them.
