About Me

Hello! I'm John Vincent Barnachea, a graduate of BSIT major in System Development at PHINMA-University of Pangasinan. I'm a passionate developer who never stops learning new things, and I have almost 2 years of work experience in WordPress development.

On October 2020, I was assigned as Operations Team Manager at Google Developer Student Club PHINMA UPang. Powered by Google Developers, it is a group of students from PHINMA University of Pangasinan whose aim is to bridge the gap between theory and practical application for student developers. Alongside the 1,266+ other GDSC chapters in 106 countries around the world, it is part of a global community supported by Google Developers. GDSC PHINMA UPang is also the only university-wide organization in PHINMA UPang that is officially recognized by Google. This community that I started has successfully organized multiple events to help aspiring developers get to know and learn about the different Google developer technologies available to them.
